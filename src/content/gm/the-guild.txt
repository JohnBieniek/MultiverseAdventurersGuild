Multiverse Adventurers Guild's Mission Statement:

To map and preserve timelines in flux.

The Guild has two jobs. Chart world histories and protect timelines.

The Guild is at odds with the Multiverse Preservation Society. For example, the Society believed T-Kat Uma of the Nima Prefecture was causing a divergent timeline, so they assassinated Him. The Guild felt T-Kat was central to the actual timeline, so they prevented the assassination.
