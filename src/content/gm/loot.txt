Treasure

Some people would argue that loot is the whole point of adventuring. This is a challenge in this system because money doesn't exist. This is handled in 2 ways. The first is through Credits, which give Heroes flexible resources during a mission. The second is treasure, which covers items found during the adventure.

Treasure is a party resource. This means any player can use a treasure. It is not considered to be held by any one Hero.

Searching for treasure, analyzing treasure, travel, healing, and door actions are covered under Adventures.
