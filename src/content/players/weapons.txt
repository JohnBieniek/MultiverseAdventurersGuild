Silencers - These increase the concealability by one step. A silencer on a pistol becomes Observation 15S. The damage decreases by one step. A sniper rifle would become a d8 weapon. A pistol becomes d4-1.
Concealment - Add the sneak skill to the TN for concealment. If you have a Sneak skill of (+2), you conceal an SMG on 17S.
The philosophy behind damage is that Heroes should be rewarded for fighting at Melee ranges because of the increased risk. Melee damage is slightly more serious than ranged damage.
