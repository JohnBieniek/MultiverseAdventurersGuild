Skills work with Stats to show what you are good at. Every Hero has every skill, but bonuses show specialization.

Assign: +2, +2, +1, +1, +1, 0, 0, 0, -1

Attack (Str or Dex): Used for every attack or offensive Talent. You buy it once and use it for melee and ranged attacks. Every Hero starts with a +1 modifier in either Melee Attack or Ranged Attack. Choose one when creating the Hero. Improve that modifier one step at a time: +1 costs 1 XP, +2 costs 4 XP, +3 costs 9 XP, and +4 costs 16 XP. Each increase is purchased separately.
Example Items: Grip-Memory Combat Gloves | Ricochet Calculator Lens | Employer-Agnostic Smartlink | Echoing Temple Handwraps | Knuckle-Plated Smart Gloves

Athletics (Str, Dex, or End): Climbing, jumping, swimming, grappling, resisting knockback, enduring physical hazards, and other physical tasks not covered by another skill.
Example Items: Climbing Claws and Silk Line | Climber’s Living-Vine Harness | Patrol-Grade Pursuit Boots | All-Terrain Broadcast Boots | Campaign-Worn Marching Boots

Influence (Cha): Intimidate, persuade, interrogate, acting, impersonation
Example Items: Restraint and Warrant Kit | Retired Detective Badge | Whisper Coaching Earpiece | Talking Drum | Tea Set of Patient Questions

Knowledge (Edu): Learned topics, research, magic theory.
Example Items: Pocket Omnilibrary | Illuminated Prayer Book | Black-Market Appraiser’s Handbook | Dog-Eared Procedure Handbook | Taboo-Knot Cord

Observation (Intuition): Noticing details, perception
Example Items: Targeting Monocle | Forensic Field Kit | Weatherwise Acorn Charm | Deja-Vu Packet Analyzer | Spirit-Track Moccasins

Outdoors (Intuition): Tracking, survival, navigation
Example Items: Mantle of Living Leaves | Seed-Bomb Bandolier | Fugitive-Scent Sampler | Smoke-Reading War Paint | Graffiti-Route AR Mask

Sneak (Dex): Stealth, sleight of hand, infiltration, disguise, and acting
Example Items: Chameleon-Lined Coat | Boots of Elvenkind | Adaptive Camouflage Cloak | Magnetic Rooftop Tabi | False-Bottom Cargo Coat

Technology (Edu): Hacking, medicine, devices, engineering, disable traps, Matrix search
Example Items: Professional Lock Roll | Spell-Geometry Monocle | Sleeve of Balanced Tools | Gunsmith’s Personal Toolkit | Ghostline Neural Implant

Vehicle (Dex): Driving a car, piloting a starship, riding a horse
Example Items: Overclocked Control Yoke | Dust-Trail Riding Boots | Route-Scrambler Nav Unit | Corner-Predicting Visor | Engine-Song Diagnostic Implant

Now choose your least important skill and write in a (-1). Pick the next 3 skills that are the least important and write in a (0). The remaining skills are (+1)."
