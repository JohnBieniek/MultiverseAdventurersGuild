Your defenses protect you from harm:

AV (Armor Value) = 11 + Defense bonus + (Armor * 2)

Athletics = Defense bonus + Str + Dex + End

Ego = Defense bonus + Intuition + Education + Charisma
These numbers are added to rolls used to resist attacks or harmful effects. Athletics provides a skill for physical tasks not covered by other skills, such as climbing. Athletic and Ego can act as AV by adding the Score of each with a defensive stat.
