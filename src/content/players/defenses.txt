Your defenses protect you from harm and harmful effects.

Defense: 11 + Defense bonus + (Defense rating x 2)

Resilience: Defense bonus + Strength + Dexterity + Endurance

Ego: Defense bonus + Intuition + Education + Charisma

Defense is the target number enemies roll against when they try to hit you.

Resilience is used to resist physical danger, poison, pain, forced movement, exhaustion, and other body-based effects.

Ego is used to resist fear, influence, mental attacks, illusions, possession, and other willpower-based effects.

Athletics remains a normal skill for physical tasks such as climbing, jumping, swimming, and other movement challenges. It is not one of your three defenses.
