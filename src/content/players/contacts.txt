Contacts are NPCs you know who can help you with information, equipment, favors, introductions, cover stories, and off-the-books services. You may choose some now and fill in others as you play. Contacts can exist anywhere in the Multiverse; if you have a bartender contact, the GM can place a similar bartender in any setting.

You begin with 3 + Charisma Contacts.

Alchemist - A field chemist, potion-maker, and reagent expert who can turn strange ingredients into useful answers. Alchemists are especially valuable when a mission leaves the party poisoned, cursed by contact, covered in unknown residue, or carrying something that should not be opened indoors.
Category: Medical and technical contacts
Example Name: Arlen Brisk
Expertise: Brews potions, identifies reagents, neutralizes chemical hazards, and improvises field mixtures.
