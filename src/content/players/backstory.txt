Building a
Heroic Backstory
This section is designed to help you flesh out the personality of your Hero. It can be used in any setting. This helps with roleplaying.
What was the Hero's greatest success and greatest failure?
What are their Spiritual or religious beliefs? Feel free to create your own religion based on setting.
How does the Hero see themself?Are they a humble candle maker who took up the sword? Are you a noble person who became a con artist?
What are your beliefs regarding good and evil? Which do you consider yourself to be? Why?
What was your family like? Large or small. How did family members make a living?
Describe your appearance.
What is your favorite weapon?
How do people respond to your Hero? Do they see them as reckless? Slow to respond to threats?
Who are your enemies? Why? Describe them.
What motivates your Hero?
Who influenced the Hero?
How do you respond to a threat? Do you immediately go on the offensive? Defensive?
What is your Hero afraid of?
Your Hero has access to all skills. Is there skill(s) that he lacks? Maybe he is good with guns but just hates and won't use shotguns.
Describe your Hero's best moment for what he became known for
Does your Hero have a signature or specific look?
